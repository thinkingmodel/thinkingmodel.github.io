Abhinav Thorat

Thinking at the intersection of silicon and stars.

A curious mind with over years of experience at the intersection of machines and meaning. Abhinav thinks in systems, whether that's the architecture of a neural network, the structure of a galaxy, or the invisible forces that shape why people do what they do.

This blog is where those questions live, a space for writing that moves between the technical and the philosophical, the ancient and the emergent. Equal parts research notebook, observatory, and commonplace book.
